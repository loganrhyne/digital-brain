# Every Life Is on Fire

**Author:** Jeremy England
**Publisher:** Basic Books
**Published:** September 15, 2020
**ISBN:** 9781541699014
**Pages:** 272
**Started Reading:** January 2, 2026

## About

A groundbreaking exploration of how thermodynamics explains the origins of life. Jeremy England presents his highly publicized theory known as dissipative adaptation, offering a physics-based perspective on how living things emerge from non-living matter.

**About the Author:** Jeremy England is senior director in artificial intelligence at GlaxoSmithKline, principal research scientist at Georgia Tech, and former Thomas D. and Virginia W. Cabot career development associate professor of physics at MIT. He was a Rhodes scholar, a Hertz fellow, and named one of Forbes "30 Under 30 Rising Stars of Science."
