**Finished Reading:** January 1, 2026

## About

An exploration of the history of cell biology and its profound impact on modern medicine, written by the Pulitzer Prize-winning author of "The Emperor of All Maladies." The book traces the discovery of cells and how understanding these fundamental units of life has revolutionized our approach to treating disease.

**Awards:**

- 2023 Chautauqua Prize
