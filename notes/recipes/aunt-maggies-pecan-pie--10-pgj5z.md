## Ingredients

### Aunt Maggie's Original Recipe
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 cup pure maple syrup
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups coarsely chopped pecans
- Unbaked pie shell

### CSO's Tinkered Version (for 10-inch shell)
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 1/4 cups maple syrup
- 1/3 cup butter
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups pecans

## Instructions
1. Beat together eggs and sugar(s).
2. Whisk in maple syrup, butter, salt and vanilla.
3. Add coarsely chopped pecans.
4. Pour into unbaked pie shell.
5. Bake at 400째F for 10 minutes.
6. Reduce temperature to 350째F and bake 35 minutes longer.
