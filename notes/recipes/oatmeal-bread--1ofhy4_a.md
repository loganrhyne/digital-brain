# Oatmeal Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Oatmeal Bread.
- [claim] A hearty bread with oats, bran, and maple syrup.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1.75 cups lukewarm water
- 1 cup whole milk
- 0.5 cup pure maple syrup
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1 tbsp salt
- 0.25 cup neutral oil
- 0.5 cup oat bran
- 1/3 cup wheat bran
- 1.5 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
- 0.5 cup whole wheat flour
- 4.25 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine water, milk, maple syrup, yeast, salt, oil, brans, oats, and flours. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Place in greased loaf pans.
3. Rest for 1 hour 20 minutes.
4. Bake at 350Â°F with steam for 50 minutes until golden brown.
