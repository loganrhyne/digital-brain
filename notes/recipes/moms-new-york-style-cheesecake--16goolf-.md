# Mom's New York-Style Cheesecake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Mom's New York-Style Cheesecake.
- [claim] Source: Food for Thoughts, Junior League of Birmingham.
- [claim] Extra egg whites can be saved for scrambled eggs.
- [claim] Requires 10-hour refrigeration.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 0.5 cup butter, softened (113g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 0.25 cup sugar
- 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour (120g)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 0.5 tsp grated lemon rind

### Filling
- 5 (8-oz) packages cream cheese, softened (1134g)
- 1.5 tsp vanilla
- 0.25 cup whipping cream
- 5 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 cups sugar (400g)
- 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 0.5 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Blend all crust ingredients together.
2. Press half the mixture on bottom of greased 8-inch springform pan.
3. Press remaining mixture on sides of pan.
4. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 6-8 minutes.
5. Beat softened cream cheese, vanilla, and whipping cream together.
6. Add eggs and egg yolks one at a time, beating well after each addition.
7. Slowly add sugar, flour, and salt. Blend well.
8. Pour into prepared pan.
9. Bake at 475°F (250°C) for 10 minutes.
10. Reduce to 200°F (95°C) and bake 1.5 hours.
11. Allow to cool in oven.
12. Refrigerate for 10 hours before serving.
