# Marianna's Peach Ice Cream

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Marianna's Peach Ice Cream.
- [claim] Makes 3 quarts (12 cups).
- [claim] Uses fresh peaches blended with sugar.
- [claim] Taste for sweetness and adjust before churning.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 1/2 cups fresh peaches
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 small can Pet evaporated milk
- 1 1/2 tablespoon vanilla
- Half and Half to make 3 quarts total (about 6-7 cups)
- Small amount of salt

## Instructions
1. Put 3 eggs and 1 cup milk in blender. Blend and remove to a large bowl.
2. Put 3 1/2 cups fresh peaches and 1 cup sugar in blender. Blend.
3. Add peach mixture to bowl and mix.
4. Add 1 small can Pet evaporated milk and 1 1/2 tablespoon vanilla.
5. Add enough Half and Half to make 3 quarts (12 cups) total.
6. Add small amount of salt.
7. Taste for sweetness and adjust if needed.
8. Churn in ice cream freezer.
