# Veggie Burgers

- [claim] Boil lentils for about 25 minutes until falling apart.
- [claim] Cook bulgur by adding to boiling water and turning off heat - let cook until water absorbed.

- [claim] Cook mushrooms until soft, about 10 minutes.

## Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cup dried brown lentils
- 2 1/4 cup bulgur
- 6 tbsp vegetable oil
- 7 1/2 tsp table salt
- 3 large onions, finely chopped
- 1 1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
- 1 1/2 cup leek or scallion, finely chopped
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 3 lb white mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 3 cup unsalted cashews
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 6 cups panko
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Bring lentils to boil in 9 cups of water and a pinch of salt. Reduce to simmer and cook for about 25 minutes, until falling apart.
2. Drain lentils in a mesh strainer, dry and set aside.
3. While lentils are cooking, boil 6 cups of water for bulgur, add bulgur and turn off heat. Let it cook until water is absorbed. Put bulgur aside.
4. In a large pan, heat oil. Add onions, celery, garlic and leek or scallion, and cook for about 8 minutes. Set aside.
5. Add mushrooms to the pan and cook until soft, about 10 minutes.
6. In a food processor, process cashews until fine.
7. Combine cashews, lentils, and bulgur with cooled vegetables and cashews. Add mayonnaise.
8. Food process (pulse) in batches until mixture is cohesive but roughly textured.
9. Stir in panko, and salt and pepper to taste.
10. Form into patties.
11. Cook on the grill or in a pan, about 5 minutes per side.
12. Serve with favorite fixings.
