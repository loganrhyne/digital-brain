## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 cup butter (227g)
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg yolk
- 6 bars of milk chocolate
- Nuts (optional, for topping)

## Instructions
1. Cream butter and brown sugar together.
2. Beat in the egg yolk, salt, and vanilla.
3. Stir in the flour.
4. Pat on greased 5x10 pan.
5. Bake at 350째F/177째C for 15-20 minutes.
6. While hot, melt Hershey bars on top and spread.
7. Sprinkle nuts over bars as desired.
