# Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies.
- [claim] Helen's recipe combining oatmeal and chocolate chips.
- [claim] Bake at 350°F for 9-11 minutes.
- [claim] Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
- [claim] Can use 1-2 cups chocolate chips depending on preference.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup butter (227g)
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups oatmeal
- 1-2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl (or sifter).
2. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until creamy.
3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
4. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
5. Stir in oatmeal and then chocolate chips.
6. Drop by rounded spoonful onto ungreased baking sheets.
7. Bake at 350°F for 9-11 minutes.
