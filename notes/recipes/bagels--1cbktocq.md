# Bagels

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Bagels.
- [claim] Traditional boiled-then-baked method.
- [claim] Hole should be 3x diameter of the dough ball.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tbsp sugar
- 6.25 cups bread flour

### Boiling Pot
- 8 quarts water
- 0.25 cup sugar
- 1 tsp baking soda

### Toppings
- Poppy or sesame seeds

## Instructions
1. Combine water, yeast, salt, sugar, and flour. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Preheat oven to 400°F.
3. Portion dough into 3-ounce (peach-size) balls. Cover with plastic wrap and rest 20 minutes.
4. Prepare boiling pot: boil water, reduce to simmer, add sugar and baking soda.
5. Form bagels by poking hole through center - hole should be 3x diameter of dough.
6. Boil bagels (don't let them touch): simmer 2 minutes, flip, simmer 1 more minute.
7. Remove and place on lightly floured towel.
8. Top with seeds if desired.
9. Bake at 400°F with steam for 20 minutes.
