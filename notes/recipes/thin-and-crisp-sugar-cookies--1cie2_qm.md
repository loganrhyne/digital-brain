# Thin and Crisp Sugar Cookies

## Observations

- [claim] Makes about 12 dozen cookies.
- [claim] Uses special technique of pressing with glass dipped in oil then sugar.
- [claim] Requires three cookie sheets for efficient baking rotation.

## Ingredients
- 1 stick sweet butter (unsalted) (1/2 cup), at room temperature
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar (plus more for rolling)
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil (canola)
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325째F (350째F in New Mexico for elevation).
2. Mix butter and granulated sugar together until very smooth and creamy.
3. Add vegetable oil, mix until very smooth and creamy.
4. Add egg, mix until very smooth and creamy.
5. Add powdered sugar, mix until very smooth and creamy.
6. Add vanilla, mix until very smooth and creamy.
7. In a separate bowl, mix 1/2 cup flour with baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt.
8. Add dry mixture to butter mixture.
9. Add remaining 2 cups flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing until smooth.
10. Roll dough into balls the size of a large grape. Place 12 per cookie sheet.
11. Take a glass (about 3" across, smooth bottom), press into bowl of dough to coat with oil film.
12. Dip glass in bowl of granulated sugar (sugar will cling to oil).
13. Use glass to press each ball flat. May require two pressings with more sugar added each time.
14. Bake first tray for 5 minutes. Edges should show slightest hint of browning.
15. If not cooked, add 30 second intervals as needed.
16. Rotate: One tray in oven, one cooling, one being prepped.
