## Ingredients

### Crust
- Graham cracker crumbs
- Sugar
- 5+ tbsp melted butter

### Filling
- Cream cheese
- Salt
- Sugar
- Sour cream
- Lemon juice
- Vanilla
- Egg yolks
- Whole eggs

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325째F, rack in lower-middle position.
2. Combine crumbs and sugar, add 5 tbsp melted butter. Brush pan with remaining butter.
3. Press crumbs into pan bottom. Bake until fragrant and browning, about 13 minutes. Cool on rack.

5. Beat cream cheese until softened. Add salt and half the sugar, beat until combined. Add remaining sugar.
6. Add sour cream, lemon juice, and vanilla. Add yolks, then whole eggs two at a time, scraping bowl between additions.
7. Brush pan sides with remaining butter. Set pan on rimmed baking sheet.
8. Pour filling into crust. Bake 10 minutes.
9. Reduce to 200째F (don't open door). Bake until center registers 150째F, about 1.5 hours.
10. Cool 5 minutes, run knife around edge. Cool 2.5-3 hours until barely warm.
11. Wrap and refrigerate at least 3 hours.
12. Let stand 30 minutes at room temperature before serving.
