# Banana Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Banana Bread.
- [claim] Helen's classic banana bread recipe.
- [claim] Uses 3 large ripe bananas.
- [claim] Bake for 1 hour at 350°F.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 - 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 large ripe bananas
- 1/4 cup nuts
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda

## Instructions
1. Cream butter and sugar. Add beaten eggs.
2. Mash bananas and stir into wet mixture.
3. Sift in dry ingredients.
4. Add vanilla.
5. Pour into a well greased, floured pan.
6. Bake for 1 hour at 350°F.
