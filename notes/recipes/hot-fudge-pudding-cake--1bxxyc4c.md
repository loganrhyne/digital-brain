# Hot Fudge Pudding Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Hot Fudge Pudding Cake.
- [claim] Helen's self-saucing chocolate pudding cake.
- [claim] Pour boiling water onto layered mixture - creates sauce during baking.
- [claim] Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Cake Batter
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup milk
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1/4 cup cocoa

### Sauce Layer
- 2 tablespoons cocoa
- 2 tablespoons instant coffee
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 3/4 cups boiling water

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking powder, cocoa (1/4 cup), granulated sugar, and salt in a small bowl.
2. Stir in milk and oil.
3. Spread batter in 9x9 baking pan.
4. Mix brown sugar, cocoa (2 Tbsp), and instant coffee and pour evenly onto batter.
5. Pour the boiling water onto the layered mixture.
6. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
