# 100% Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for 100% Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread.
- [claim] Uses honey and milk for a softer, richer whole wheat loaf.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1.5 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 cups lukewarm milk
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 cup honey
- 5 tbsp neutral oil
- 6 2/3 cups whole wheat flour

## Instructions
1. Combine water, milk, yeast, salt, honey, oil, and flour. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Place in greased loaf pans.
3. Rest for 1 hour 40 minutes.
4. Bake at 350Â°F with steam for 55 minutes until golden brown.
