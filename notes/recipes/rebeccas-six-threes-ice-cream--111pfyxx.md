# Rebecca's Six Three's Ice Cream

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Rebecca's Six Three's Ice Cream.
- [claim] Family recipe named for the pattern of ingredients: 3 cups, 3 cups, 3 of each fruit.
- [claim] Simple mix-and-churn recipe.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups milk
- 3 cups light cream (Half and Half)
- 3 cups sugar
- Juice of 3 lemons
- Juice of 3 oranges
- 3 mashed bananas
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together.
2. Churn in ice cream freezer.
