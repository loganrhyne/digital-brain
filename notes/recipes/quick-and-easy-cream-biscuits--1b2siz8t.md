## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 1.5 cups heavy cream

## Instructions
1. Adjust oven rack to upper-middle position and heat oven to 450Â°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in medium bowl.
3. Stir in the cream with a wooden spoon until dough forms, about 30 seconds.
4. Turn dough out onto lightly floured counter and gather into a ball. Knead briefly until smooth, about 30 seconds.
5. Shape dough into a 3/4-inch-thick circle.
6. Cut biscuits into rounds or wedges. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet.
7. Bake until golden brown, about 15 minutes, rotating baking sheet halfway through.
