# Chocolate Chip Flying Saucers

## Observations

- [claim] No nuts, no frills - pure bliss.

- [claim] 243 calories per cookie, 13g fat, 32g carbs, 3g protein.

## Ingredients
- 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup packed dark brown sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 0.75 tsp fine salt
- 0.5 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup white whole-wheat flour (such as King Arthur)
- 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Arrange oven racks in upper and lower thirds. Preheat to 375Â°F. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment.
2. Cream butter and sugars at medium-high speed until fluffy, about 5 minutes.
3. Add eggs, vanilla, salt, and baking soda. Beat at medium speed until incorporated, 1-2 minutes.
4. Add flours and mix at low speed until just blended. Stir in chocolate chips.
5. Drop 6 heaping tablespoons of dough on each sheet, spaced evenly.
6. Bake 7 minutes, switch and rotate pans, bake 5-7 more minutes until evenly golden.
7. Cool on pans 1-2 minutes, then transfer to racks. Repeat with remaining dough.
