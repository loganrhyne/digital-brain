# Beignets (Donuts)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Beignets (Donuts).
- [claim] Uses pre-made enriched dough (Brioche or Challah).
- [claim] Oil temperature is critical: 360-370°F.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 pound Brioche or Challah dough
- Vegetable oil for deep frying
- Powdered sugar, cinnamon, chocolate powder, etc. for dusting

### Special Equipment
- Deep saucepan for frying
- Slotted spoon
- Paper towels
- Candy thermometer

## Instructions
1. Form dough into ball.
2. Roll into 1/2-inch thick rectangle.
3. Cut into 2-inch squares with pizza cutter.
4. Rest for 20 minutes.
5. Fill saucepan with at least 3 inches vegetable oil. Heat to 360-370°F.
6. Drop in squares 2 or 3 at a time with plenty of space.
7. Flip after 2 minutes, then cook another minute.
8. Remove from oil and place on paper towels to drain.
9. Dust with powdered sugar, cinnamon, chocolate powder, etc.
