# Fish & Vegetable Curry

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Fish & Vegetable Curry.
- [claim] Quick and simple curry ready in about 10 minutes.
- [claim] Heat coconut milk and curry paste for 2 minutes until combined.
- [claim] Simmer fish and vegetables covered for 4-5 minutes until fish is cooked.
- [claim] Serve with fresh coriander leaves.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 400g (14oz) white fish fillets, cut into thick slices
- 400g coconut milk
- 2 tbsp red curry paste
- 2 carrots, cut into thin strips
- 2 cups red cabbage, thinly sliced
- Fresh coriander

## Instructions
1. Place coconut milk and red curry paste into a pan on medium heat for 2 minutes, stirring until combined.
2. Add fish, carrots and red cabbage. Cover and simmer for 4-5 minutes, or until fish has cooked.
3. Serve with fresh coriander leaves.
