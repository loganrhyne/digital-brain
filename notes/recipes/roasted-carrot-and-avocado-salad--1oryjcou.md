# Roasted Carrot and Avocado Salad

## Observations

## Ingredients
- 1 pound carrots, scrubbed or peeled and cut into 2-inch segments
- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- Coarse salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 an avocado, pitted and sliced
- Juice of half a lemon

## Instructions
1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
2. Toss the carrot chunks in a medium bowl with 2 tablespoons of the olive oil, cumin and as generous a helping of salt and pepper as you like.
3. Spread them on a roasting sheet and roast for about 20 minutes, or until tender and browned. Roasting time will vary depending on the thickness of your carrots. Heftier chunks may take over 30 minutes.
4. Once the carrots are roasted, arrange them on a serving platter with slices of avocado on top.
5. Drizzle the salad with the last tablespoon of olive oil, lemon juice and extra salt and pepper, if it needs it.
6. Eat immediately.
