# Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread (Inspired by Chris Kimball)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Whole Wheat Sandwich Bread.
- [claim] Inspired by Chris Kimball's approach.
- [claim] Uses honey and butter for a richer loaf.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1 tbsp + 1 tsp salt
- 0.25 cup honey
- 0.25 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 0.25 cup rye flour
- 0.5 cup wheat germ
- 2.75 cups whole wheat flour
- 2.75 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine water, yeast, salt, honey, melted butter, and flours. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Place in greased loaf pans.
3. Rest for 1 hour 40 minutes.
4. Bake at 400Â°F with steam for 55 minutes until golden brown.
