# Maple Pecan Ice Cream

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Maple Pecan Ice Cream.
- [claim] Makes about 1/2 liter (1/2 quart).
- [claim] The darker the maple syrup, the better.
- [claim] Custard base can be prepared up to 12 hours ahead.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 240 ml (1 cup) milk
- 1 tbsp raw cane sugar
- 100g (1/3 cup + 1 tbsp) crème fraîche or heavy cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 80 ml (1/3 cup) maple syrup, dark preferred
- 1 good pinch sea salt
- 75g (3/4 cup) pecans, coarsely chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat milk and sugar in medium saucepan over medium heat.
2. Put cream in mixing bowl with pouring lip, set fine-mesh sieve over it.
3. Beat egg yolks lightly in heatproof bowl.
4. When milk is steaming, pour slowly into yolks while whisking constantly.
5. Pour back into saucepan and cook, stirring constantly with wooden spoon, until mixture thickens and coats back of spoon (leaves neat path when finger drawn across).
6. Pour through sieve into cream. Whisk in maple syrup and salt.
7. Place ice cubes in larger bowl, nest custard bowl in it, add cold water (ice bath).
8. Whisk until cool, cover, and refrigerate until chilled.
9. Churn in ice cream machine, adding pecans when almost set.
