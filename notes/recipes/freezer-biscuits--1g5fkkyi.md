# Freezer Biscuits

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Freezer Biscuits.
- [claim] Source: America's Test Kitchen, February 2008.
- [claim] Can be frozen for up to 1 month. Do not thaw before baking.
- [claim] Dough cannot be held in refrigerator or it won't bake well.

## Ingredients
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups heavy cream

## Instructions
1. Line rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in large bowl.
3. Stir in cream with wooden spoon until dough forms, about 30 seconds.
4. Turn dough onto lightly floured counter, gather into ball. Knead briefly until smooth, about 60 seconds.
5. Pat dough into 3/4-inch thick circle.
6. Cut biscuits using 2.5-inch round cutter. Lay on prepared sheet, spaced 1/2 inch apart.
7. Gather scraps, reknead briefly, pat again, and cut more biscuits. Repeat once more.

### To Freeze
8. Wrap baking sheet tightly with greased plastic wrap. Freeze until solid, about 6 hours.
9. Transfer frozen biscuits to zipper-lock bag. Freeze up to 1 month.

10. Preheat oven to 450Â°F with rack in upper-middle position.
11. Place frozen biscuits on parchment-lined sheet, 2 inches apart.
12. Bake until puffed and golden brown, 20-25 minutes.
