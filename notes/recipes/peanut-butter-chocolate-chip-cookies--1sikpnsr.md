# Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients
- 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar (plus more for coating)
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 large eggs
- 1 3/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt (in a sifter if available).
2. Beat butter and peanut butter before mixing in sugars. Beat for 3 minutes more.
3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating for 1 minute after each addition.
4. Stir in dry ingredients until thoroughly mixed.
5. Add chocolate chips.
6. Pour some granulated sugar into a small bowl.
7. Roll spoonfuls of the dough into balls and coat with sugar by rolling them in the bowl.
8. Place on cookie sheet and flatten with tines of a fork to create crisscross indentations.
9. Bake at 350°F for 12 minutes.
