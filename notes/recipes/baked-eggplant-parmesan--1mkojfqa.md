# Baked Eggplant Parmesan

## Ingredients
- Olive oil, for baking sheets
- 2 large eggs
- 3/4 cup plain dry breadcrumbs
- 3/4 cup finely grated Parmesan, plus 2 tablespoons for topping
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- Coarse salt and ground pepper
- 2 large eggplants (2 1/2 pounds total), peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
- 6 cups Chunky Tomato Sauce
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375째F. Brush 2 baking sheets with oil; set aside.
2. In a wide, shallow bowl, whisk together eggs and 2 tablespoons water.
3. In another bowl, combine breadcrumbs, 3/4 cup Parmesan, oregano, and basil; season with salt and pepper.
4. Dip eggplant slices in egg mixture, letting excess drip off, then dredge in breadcrumb mixture, coating well; place on baking sheets.
5. Bake until golden brown on bottom, 20 to 25 minutes.
6. Turn slices; continue baking until browned on other side, 20 to 25 minutes more.
7. Remove from oven; raise oven heat to 400째F.
8. Spread 2 cups sauce in a 9-by-13-inch baking dish.
9. Arrange half the eggplant in dish; cover with 2 cups sauce, then 1/2 cup mozzarella.
10. Repeat with remaining eggplant, sauce, and mozzarella; sprinkle with remaining 2 tablespoons Parmesan.
11. Bake until sauce is bubbling and cheese is melted, 15 to 20 minutes.
12. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
