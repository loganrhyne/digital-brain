# Cheesy Eggplant Bake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Cheesy Eggplant Bake.
- [claim] Adapted from All Recipes.
- [claim] Serves 6.
- [claim] Salt eggplant slices for half an hour to draw out moisture and bitterness.
- [claim] Rinse under cold water and pat dry after salting.
- [claim] Cook eggplant rounds about 2 minutes per side until browned.
- [claim] Bake covered at 350°F for 25 minutes, then uncovered with cheese for 25-30 minutes.
- [claim] Serve to applause.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 medium eggplant, peeled
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon garlic salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1 egg + 2 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1 large green pepper, chopped
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
- 6 ounces part skim mozzarella cheese, shredded

## Instructions
1. Slice eggplant crosswise into 1/4-inch rounds.
2. Arrange rounds in a colander in your sink and sprinkle salt all over them. Walk away for half an hour.
3. When finished, rinse under cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
4. While eggplant is sitting, combine bread crumbs, garlic salt, and pepper in a shallow bowl or on a plate.
5. In a separate shallow bowl, whisk eggs.
6. When eggplant is done, douse each slice in the egg mixture. Then dip in the bread crumb mixture to coat. Shake off any excess.
7. In a large skillet, heat 1/2 tablespoon oil over medium-high heat.
8. Cook a few rounds until browned, about 2 minutes per side. When finished, arrange in 13x9-inch baking dish. Repeat for second batch.
9. Preheat oven to 350°F.
10. Heat last 1 tablespoon oil in same skillet over medium-high heat.
11. Add green pepper, onion, and mushrooms. Cook until onion is softening and pepper is crisp/tender, about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
12. Top eggplant with mixture. Add tomatoes on top of that, spreading evenly.
13. Cover with tin foil and bake 25 minutes.
14. Remove from oven and take off tin foil. Sprinkle cheese on top and bake another 25 or 30 minutes, until cheese is melted and a little brown.
15. Serve.
