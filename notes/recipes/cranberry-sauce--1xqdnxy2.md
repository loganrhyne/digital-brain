# Cranberry Sauce

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Cranberry Sauce.
- [claim] Helen's recipe from Deloitte 2014 Thanksgiving Recipe PDF (Jim Rose).
- [claim] Simmer gently 20 minutes until berries are tender and crack.
- [claim] Fantastic on turkey sandwiches or mixed in vanilla yogurt.
- [claim] Cranberries available at Trader Joe's.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 (12oz) package cranberries
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup red wine
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 large apple, peeled, cored, and cut in medium-sized pieces
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions
1. Rinse and drain berries.
2. Combine all ingredients in a large pot.
3. Bring to a soft boil.
4. Simmer gently 20 minutes, or until berries are tender and crack.
5. Remove from heat.
6. Cool to room temperature.
7. Store in refrigerator covered.
