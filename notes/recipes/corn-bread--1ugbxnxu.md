# Corn Bread

## Ingredients
- All-purpose flour
- Yellow cornmeal
- Baking powder
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Brown sugar
- Frozen corn kernels, thawed
- Buttermilk
- Eggs
- Melted butter

## Instructions
1. Adjust oven rack to middle position; heat oven to 400°F. Spray 8-inch square baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in medium bowl; set aside.
3. In food processor or blender, process brown sugar, thawed corn kernels, and buttermilk until combined, about 5 seconds.
4. Add eggs and process until well combined (corn lumps will remain), about 5 seconds longer.
5. Make well in center of dry ingredients; pour wet ingredients into well.
6. Begin folding dry into wet, giving mixture only a few turns to barely combine.
7. Add melted butter and continue folding until dry ingredients are just moistened.
8. Pour batter into prepared dish; smooth surface.
9. Bake until deep golden brown and toothpick comes out clean, 25-35 minutes.
10. Cool on wire rack 10 minutes; invert, turn right side up, continue cooling about 10 minutes. Serve warm.
