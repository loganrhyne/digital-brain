# Baked Shrimp in Tomato Feta Sauce

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Baked Shrimp in Tomato Feta Sauce.
- [claim] Uses an oven-proof skillet for cooking and baking.
- [claim] Cook onions until softened (3-5 minutes) before adding garlic.
- [claim] Simmer tomatoes for 5-10 minutes until juices thicken a bit.
- [claim] Bake at 425°F for 10-12 minutes until shrimp are cooked through.
- [claim] Can serve with crusty French or Italian bread, pasta, or rice.
- [claim] Serves 4.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 (14.5-ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
- 1 tbsp minced fresh dill or 1 tsp dried dill
- 1 to 1 1/4 pounds medium-sized raw shrimp, peeled and deveined (can leave tails on), thaw if frozen
- Pinch of salt, more to taste
- Pinch black pepper, more to taste
- 3 ounces feta cheese (about 2/3 cup, crumbled)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Heat oil in a large, oven-proof skillet on medium-high heat. Add the onions and cook until softened, 3-5 minutes.
3. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds more.
4. Add the tomatoes and bring to a simmer, reduce heat and let simmer for 5-10 minutes, until the juices thicken a bit.
5. Remove from heat. Stir in the herbs, shrimp, feta cheese, and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Place pan in oven and bake, uncovered, until shrimp are cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.
7. Serve immediately with crusty French or Italian loaf bread, pasta, or rice.
