# Aunt Catherine's Overnight Cinnamon Rolls

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Aunt Catherine's Overnight Cinnamon Rolls.
- [claim] Family recipe - dough chills overnight in refrigerator.
- [claim] Can divide dough into 4 pieces for smaller rolls.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 2 (1/4-oz) envelopes active dry yeast
- 1 cup warm water (100-110°F)
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 cup Crisco shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 large eggs
- 6 cups all-purpose flour

### Filling
- 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
- 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tbsp butter, melted

### Icing
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tbsp milk
- 0.5 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. Combine yeast, 1 cup warm water, and 2 tsp sugar in measuring cup; let stand 5 minutes.
2. Place shortening, 1 cup sugar, and salt in large mixing bowl.
3. Add 1 cup boiling water and beat at medium speed until smooth.
4. Add eggs and yeast mixture, beat at low speed until blended.
5. Gradually beat in flour.
6. Cover and chill for 8 hours (overnight).
7. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon together for filling.
8. Divide dough in half. Roll each portion into 12x10-inch rectangle on well-floured surface.
9. Brush dough with melted butter. Sprinkle filling over dough, leaving 1/2-inch border.
10. Roll up jelly-roll fashion starting from long side, press edges to seal.
11. Cut logs into 1-inch slices. Place in lightly greased pans, 12 rolls per pan.
12. Cover and let rise in warm place until doubled.
13. Bake at 375°F for 12-16 minutes until lightly browned.
14. Stir together icing ingredients and drizzle over warm rolls.
