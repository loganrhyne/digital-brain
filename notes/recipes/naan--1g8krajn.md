# Naan

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Naan.
- [claim] Uses existing bread dough (boule, peasant, or light whole wheat).
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/4 pound bread dough (boule, peasant, or light whole wheat)
- 2 tbsp ghee or oil
- Butter for brushing

## Instructions
1. Roll dough to uniform 1/8 inch thickness.
2. Heat cast iron skillet on high heat until water droplets skitter, add ghee/oil.
3. Drop rolled round into skillet, decrease heat to medium.
4. Flip when browning, no more than 3 minutes.
5. Cook 2-6 minutes until firm at edges.
6. Remove and brush with butter if cooked in oil.
