# Malted Hot Chocolate

- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 quart milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 4 oz semisweet chocolate, coarsely chopped
- 3/4 cup malted-milk powder

## Instructions
1. In a medium saucepan, heat milk and vanilla over medium heat until milk begins to steam (5-6 min).
2. Add chocolate and whisk until completely melted and combined.
3. Pour malted powder into a warmed serving pitcher or another saucepan.
4. Add hot chocolate mixture, whisking thoroughly until powder dissolves.
5. Divide into 4 mugs and serve immediately.
