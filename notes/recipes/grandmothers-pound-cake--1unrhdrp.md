# Grandmother's Pound Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Grandmother's Pound Cake.
- [claim] Family recipe - starts in cold oven.
- [claim] Uses both butter and Crisco for texture.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Cake
- 1 stick butter
- 1 cup Crisco
- 3 cups sugar
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour, sifted
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 1 tsp orange extract
- 0.5 tsp lemon extract
- 1 heaping tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder

### Lemon Glaze
- 2 heaping cups powdered sugar
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp water
- Rind of 1/2 lemon

## Instructions
1. Cream butter, Crisco, and sugar.
2. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each.
3. Add flour, flavorings, and milk. Mix well.
4. Pour into greased tube or bundt pan.
5. Place in COLD oven.
6. Set oven to 325Â°F and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes.
7. For glaze: mix powdered sugar, lemon juice, water, and lemon rind.
8. Pour glaze over warm cake.
