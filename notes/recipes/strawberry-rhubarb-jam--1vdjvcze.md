# Strawberry Rhubarb Jam

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Strawberry Rhubarb Jam.
- [claim] Helen's simple jam recipe using Jello.
- [claim] Boil rhubarb and sugar until rhubarb is tender (10 min).
- [claim] Mix in Jello while still boiling.
- [claim] Will thicken as it cools.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 8 cups rhubarb, cut up
- 5 cups sugar
- 2 boxes strawberry Jello
- 2 pints frozen strawberries
- Sterile jars

## Instructions
1. Boil rhubarb and sugar until the rhubarb is tender (10 min).
2. Mix in the strawberry Jello while the fruit is still boiling.
3. Add the frozen strawberries and stir.
4. Pour into jars. It will thicken as it cools.
