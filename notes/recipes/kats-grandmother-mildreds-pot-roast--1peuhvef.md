# Kat's Grandmother Mildred's Pot Roast

## Ingredients
- 3 lb single brisket (1 layer)
- 3-4 onions
- 2 carrots, peeled
- 1 cup ketchup diluted with water
- Salt and pepper
- Paprika
- Oil (if needed for sautéing)

## Instructions
1. Sear brisket on both sides starting with the fatty side. Remove from pan.
2. Coarsely chop onions and slice carrots, and sauté in the fat left over in the pan; add some oil if there isn't enough.
3. Put meat back in (over veggies); add more salt and pepper, as well as a bit of paprika.
4. Cook on med-low for 2.5 hours, turning meat to cook evenly. DO NOT ADD ANY WATER.
5. Cook until meat is fork tender.
6. Turn off the heat and allow to cool for 1/2 hour.
7. Take out and put in low dish.
8. Mash veggies with a potato masher in pot with diluted ketchup.
9. Check the seasoning and add anything that seems to not have enough.
10. Slice meat against the grain and cover with sauce.
11. Can be reheated at 300°F until hot.
