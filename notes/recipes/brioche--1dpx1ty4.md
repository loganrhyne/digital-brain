# Brioche

## Ingredients
- 1.5 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 8 eggs, lightly beaten
- 0.5 cup honey
- 1.5 cups unsalted butter, melted
- 7.5 cups all-purpose flour

### Egg Wash
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Combine lukewarm water, yeast, salt, eggs, honey, melted butter, and flour. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Place dough in greased baking tin.
3. Rest for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
4. Brush with egg wash.
5. Bake at 350Â°F for 35-40 minutes until golden brown.
