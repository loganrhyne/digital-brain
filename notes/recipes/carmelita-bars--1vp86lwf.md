# Carmelita Bars

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Carmelita Bars.
- [claim] Optional: add 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans over crust before chocolate chips.
- [claim] Can use jarred caramel sauce or make from scratch.
- [claim] Bars will firm up as they cool but should stay chewy.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 cup + 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (divided)
- 1 cup + 1/3 cup quick oats (divided)
- 0.75 cup light brown sugar
- 0.5 tsp baking soda
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.75 cup melted butter
- 0.5 cup sweetened shredded coconut
- 6 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 cup caramel sauce

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Stir together 1 cup flour, 1 cup oats, baking soda, and salt.
3. Stir in brown sugar, then melted butter, then coconut.
4. Spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray. Pat dough into even layer at bottom.
5. Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
6. Remove and sprinkle with chocolate chips. Cover with caramel sauce, spread evenly.
7. Mix remaining 2 tbsp flour and 1/3 cup oats. Sprinkle over caramel.
8. Bake for another 25 minutes.
9. Remove and let cool 5 minutes. Cut into bars.
10. Let cool 20-30 more minutes before serving.
