# Greta's Kringle

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Greta's Kringle.
- [claim] Helen's Danish pastry with almond filling.
- [claim] Pat crust out in two long strips on 13x9 inch pan.
- [claim] Spread puff pastry mixture onto crusts.
- [claim] Bake 50-60 minutes at 350°F.
- [claim] Frost when cool with almond icing and powdered sugar.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1-2 Tbsp water

### Puff Pastry
- 1 cup water
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup flour
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 tsp almond extract

### Topping
- Powdered sugar
- Almond icing

## Instructions
1. Mix crust ingredients (flour, butter, water) like a pie crust.
2. Pat out in two long strips on a 13x9 inch pan.
3. Heat water and butter to boiling, remove from heat.
4. Add 1 cup flour. Beat.
5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.
6. Add almond extract and spread onto crusts.
7. Bake 50 to 60 minutes at 350°F.
8. When cool, frost with almond icing and powdered sugar.
