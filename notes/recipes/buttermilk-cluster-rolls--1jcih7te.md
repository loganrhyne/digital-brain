# Buttermilk Cluster Rolls

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Buttermilk Cluster Rolls.
- [claim] Serves 12 adults or 4 hungry children.
- [claim] Best served with soft butter.
- [claim] Can use 9-inch cake pan but springform is easier for removal.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 28 oz (800g) all-purpose flour (about 5.5 cups)
- 20 oz (570g) buttermilk, room temperature
- 0.25 oz (7g) active dry yeast (1 package)
- 0.5 oz (14g) kosher salt (1 tablespoon)
- 1.5 oz (40g) honey (2 tablespoons)
- Vegetable spray or butter for greasing
- 1 egg (for egg wash)
- 1 tsp poppy seeds (or as desired)
- 1 tbsp melted butter, optional

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, buttermilk, yeast, salt, and honey in bowl of standing mixer.
2. Mix on medium until dough is smooth and elastic, about 10 minutes.
3. Cover and let rise until doubled in volume (2-3+ hours depending on temperature). Dough shouldn't bounce back when pressed.
4. Turn dough out onto counter and give it a good knead.
5. Divide into 12 equal portions (about 4 oz each). Form each into a tight boule by rolling on counter.
6. Spray or butter a springform pan. Fit boules into it.
7. Cover with towel and let rise for 1 hour.

9. Whisk egg until uniformly yellow. Brush rolls with egg wash and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
10. Bake for 40 minutes (internal temp 195-200째F / 90-93째C).
11. Let rest about 10 minutes before serving.
