# Guillaume's Tiramisu

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Guillaume's Tiramisu.
- [claim] Source: Guillaume Miara (family recipe from his mother).
- [claim] Best made a day in advance.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- 300g of Mascarpone
- Lady fingers biscuits (the dry ones work better)
- 100-150g of brown sugar
- Strong coffee (1.5-2 mugs worth)
- Some rum
- Unsweetened cocoa for topping

## Instructions
1. Prepare a strong espresso-like coffee (1.5-2 mugs), add rum, put in refrigerator to cool.
2. Separate yolks from whites, put yolks in a larger bowl.
3. Add sugar and mascarpone to egg yolks and whisk until smooth.
4. Add a tiny bit of salt to whites and beat well with egg beater.
5. Incorporate egg whites: add 1/3 to yolk mix and whisk, then fold in the rest gently.
6. Dip biscuits quickly on both sides in coffee (don't soak) and layer in platter.
7. Add cream layer on top, spread evenly.
8. Add another layer of dipped biscuits, then another cream layer.
9. Cover with plastic film and refrigerate overnight.
10. Before serving, dust with unsweetened cocoa.
