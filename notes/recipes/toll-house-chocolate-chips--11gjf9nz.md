# Toll House Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Toll House Chocolate Chip Cookies.
- [claim] Helen's recipe for classic Toll House chocolate chip cookies.
- [claim] Bake at 375°F for 8-10 minutes.
- [claim] Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheets.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour (288g)
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup butter (227g)
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar (150g)
- 3/4 cup brown sugar (150g)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
2. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until creamy.
3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
4. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
5. Stir in chocolate chips.
6. Drop by rounded spoonful onto ungreased baking sheets.
7. Bake at 375°F for 8-10 minutes.
