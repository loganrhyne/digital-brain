# Italian Semolina Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Italian Semolina Bread.
- [claim] Uses durum (semolina) flour for golden color and chewy texture.
- [claim] Topped with cornstarch wash and sesame seeds.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 3 cups durum (semolina) flour
- 3.25 cups all-purpose flour
- Cornstarch wash
- Sesame seeds for topping

## Instructions
1. Combine water, yeast, salt, and flours. Mix until combined.
2. Form into elongated oval free-form loaves.
3. Rest for 40 minutes.
4. Brush with cornstarch wash and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
5. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 35 minutes.
