# Refrigerator Rolls

## Ingredients
- 2 cups warm water
- 2 packages dry yeast
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 7 cups flour
- 2-3 eggs
- 1/4 cup butter

## Instructions
1. Mix the water, yeast, sugar, salt, and 3 cups of flour in a large bowl.
2. Add eggs, butter, and the remaining 4 cups of flour.
3. Cover with a damp cloth and refrigerate.
4. About 2 hours before serving, roll out in desired roll shapes.
5. Let rise two hours at room temperature.
6. Bake for 10 minutes at 375°F.
