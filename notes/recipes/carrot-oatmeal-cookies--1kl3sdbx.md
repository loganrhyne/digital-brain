# Carrot Oatmeal Cookies

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Carrot Oatmeal Cookies.
- [claim] Makes about 2 1/2 dozen cookies.
- [claim] Can experiment with different nuts/seeds after initial batch.
- [claim] Lemon zest, clarified butter, and olive oil might be ingredients to play with.
- [claim] Coconut oil brings great flavor and richness.
- [claim] Can substitute unbleached all-purpose flour if can't find whole wheat pastry flour.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- Scant 1/2 teaspoon fine grain sea salt
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2/3 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 1/2 cup real maple syrup, room temperature
- 1/2 cup unrefined (fragrant) coconut oil, warmed until just melted
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F degrees and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and oats.
3. Add the nuts and carrots.
4. In a separate smaller bowl use a whisk to combine the maple syrup, coconut oil, and ginger.
5. Add wet ingredients to the flour mixture and stir until just combined.
6. Drop onto prepared baking sheets, one level tablespoonful at a time, leaving about 2 inches between each cookie.
7. Bake in the top 1/3 of the oven for 10-12 minutes or until the cookies are golden on top and bottom.
