# Carrot Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Carrot Cake.
- [claim] Uses wholemeal flour for a heartier texture.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Cake
- 2 cups wholemeal flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 3 cups grated carrots
- 2 cups raw sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup melted butter

### Cream Cheese Icing
- 100g cream cheese
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 50g butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Mix dry ingredients together (flour, baking soda, salt, sugar, cinnamon).
3. Mix in melted butter.
4. Mix in beaten eggs.
5. Mix in grated carrots.
6. Pour into prepared pan.
7. Bake for 1 hour until toothpick comes out clean.
8. Let cool completely before frosting.
9. For icing: beat cream cheese, butter, sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Spread on cooled cake.
