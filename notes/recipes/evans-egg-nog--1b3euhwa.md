# Evans' Egg Nog

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Evans' Egg Nog.
- [claim] Helen's family recipe for eggnog with brandy and rum.
- [claim] Beat sugar with yolks until light and lemon colored.
- [claim] Add liquor drop by drop, beating constantly.
- [claim] Prepare no sooner than 24 hours before serving.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 12 eggs
- 1 quart cream
- 1 pint brandy
- Almost 1/2 pint Jamaica rum
- 12 level Tbsp sugar

## Instructions
1. Divide eggs. Beat whites and yolks separately.
2. Beat sugar with the yolks until light and lemon colored.
3. Add 1/4 of the beaten whites to the yolk/sugar mixture. Beat.
4. Combine the liquors and add drop by drop, beating constantly.
5. Whip cream and fold in.
6. Prepare no sooner than 24 hours before serving.
