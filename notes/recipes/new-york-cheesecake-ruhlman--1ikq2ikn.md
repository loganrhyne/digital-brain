# New York Cheesecake (Ruhlman Gourmet)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for New York Cheesecake.
- [claim] Adapted from Gourmet Magazine.
- [claim] Philadelphia cream cheese recommended for cheesecakes.
- [claim] Keeps covered and chilled 2 weeks.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Crumb Crust
- 8 oz graham crackers, finely ground (about 15 sheets)
- 8 tbsp (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
- 0.5 cup sugar
- 0.25 tsp salt

### Filling
- 5 (8-oz) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1.75 cups sugar
- 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest
- 1 tsp finely grated orange zest
- 5 large eggs
- 2 large egg yolks
- 0.5 tsp vanilla

### Cherry Topping (optional)
- 10 oz sweet or sour cherries, pitted
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 0.25 cup sugar
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 0.5 cup water

## Instructions
1. Mix crust ingredients, press into bottom and up sides of buttered 9.5-inch springform pan. Freeze while preparing filling.
2. Preheat oven to 550°F.
3. Beat cream cheese, sugar, flour, and zests until smooth.
4. Add vanilla, then eggs and yolks one at a time, beating on low. Scrape bowl between additions.
5. Pour filling into crust (pan will be completely full).
6. Bake 12 minutes until puffed. Watch for over-browning.
7. Reduce to 200°F and bake about 1 hour more until mostly firm with slightly wobbly center.
8. Run knife around edge. Cool completely, then chill at least 6 hours.
9. For topping: combine all ingredients in saucepan, boil 1-2 minutes. Cool completely. Spread over chilled cheesecake.
