# Rebecca's Peach (or Strawberry) Ice Cream

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Rebecca's Peach (or Strawberry) Ice Cream.
- [claim] Family recipe using food processor method.
- [claim] Works equally well with peaches or strawberries.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 quarts peaches or strawberries
- 2 cups sugar (divided: 1 cup + 1 cup)
- 4 eggs
- 0.5 cup milk
- 2.5 quarts (10 cups) half and half
- 2 tsp vanilla

## Instructions
1. Put 2 quarts of peaches or strawberries and 1 cup sugar in food processor. Blend well. Remove to large bowl.
2. Put eggs, 1 cup sugar, and 1/2 cup milk in food processor. Blend for a very long time.
3. Add egg mixture to fruit bowl and mix.
4. Add 10 cups half and half.
5. Add 2 tsp vanilla.
6. Churn in ice cream freezer according to manufacturer's instructions.
