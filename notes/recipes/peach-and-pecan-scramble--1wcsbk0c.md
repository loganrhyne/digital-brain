# Peach & Pecan Scramble

## Observations

- [claim] Source: Scott Hagnas, July 2009.
- [claim] Unusual combination but surprisingly delicious - almost like dessert for breakfast.

- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 0.5 peach, diced
- 2 tbsp chopped pecans
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp unsweetened applesauce
- 1/8 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in small skillet over medium heat.
2. Dice half of a ripe peach and chop the pecans.
3. Add peaches and pecans to skillet, stir fry 2-3 minutes until peaches soften.
4. Meanwhile, crack eggs into bowl, add applesauce and cinnamon, beat well.
5. Add egg mixture to skillet, mixing often.
6. When eggs have set, serve and enjoy.
