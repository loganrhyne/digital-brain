# Light Whole Wheat Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Light Whole Wheat Bread.
- [claim] A lighter take on whole wheat with mostly all-purpose flour.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 5.5 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine water, yeast, salt, and flours. Mix until combined.
2. Rest for 40 minutes.
3. Shape loaves and place on baking sheet.
4. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 35 minutes until golden brown.
