# Pita

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Pita bread.
- [claim] Uses pre-made bread dough (1 pound boule).
- [claim] High heat (500°F) creates the signature puff.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 pound bread dough (boule)

## Instructions
1. Roll dough into round with 1/8 inch thickness.
2. Bake at 500°F for 5-7 minutes until lightly browned and puffed.
3. Cool covered with dish cloth to keep soft.
