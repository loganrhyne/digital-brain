# Anzac Biscuits

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Anzac Biscuits.
- [claim] Traditional Australian/New Zealand cookie.
- [claim] Bake at 180°C for 15-20 minutes.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup oats
- 1 cup coconut
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 Tbsp honey
- 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
- 2 Tbsp boiling water

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, oats, coconut, and brown sugar.
2. Melt honey and butter.
3. Add baking soda and boiling water to honey-butter mixture.
4. Mix wet and dry ingredients together.
5. Bake at 180°C for 15-20 minutes.
