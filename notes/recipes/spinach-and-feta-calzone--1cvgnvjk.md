# Spinach and Feta Calzone

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Spinach and Feta Calzone.
- [claim] Uses pre-made bread dough (1 pound boule).
- [claim] No resting required before baking.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 pound bread dough (boule)
- 1 large garlic clove, minced
- 1-2 tbsp olive oil
- 0.5 cup fresh or thawed and drained frozen spinach leaves
- 1 egg
- 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese
- 0.25 tsp salt
- Freshly ground black pepper to taste
- Whole wheat flour for peel

## Instructions
1. Sauté garlic and spinach in olive oil for 2 minutes.
2. Beat egg and blend with ricotta cheese, salt, and pepper, then mix with spinach.
3. Form dough into ball, then flatten to 1/8 inch thick round.
4. Cover half the dough with filling, then seal edges with water.
5. Cut three slits all the way through the top.
6. Bake at 450°F with steam for 25 minutes.
