# Camp Stove Veggie Chili

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Camp Stove Veggie Chili.
- [claim] Serves 6 regular people or 3 really hungry campers.
- [claim] Uses textured vegetable protein (TVP) instead of meat.
- [claim] Minimum 20 minutes simmer recommended, but can be shorter if fuel is limited.
- [claim] Brown the spices with vegetables for 2-3 minutes before adding tomatoes and beans.
- [claim] Approximate calories per serving: 193.7 calories, 2.6g fat, $1.12.
- [claim] Follow with s'mores for camping experience.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 (15 oz) can black beans
- 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans
- 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 large onion, diced
- 1 medium yellow bell pepper, diced
- 1/2 orange bell pepper, diced
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup textured vegetable protein (TVP)
- 1/2 cup water (to rehydrate TVP)
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 scant tsp cayenne pepper (or a bit more)

## Instructions
1. In the oil, saut√© onions, peppers, and garlic in large saucepan until onions are soft and translucent.
2. Add sugar and spices to veggies and brown for 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes and beans to veggies and stir.
4. Mix TVP (textured vegetable protein) in water and let rehydrate for a minute or so, then add to chili.
5. Simmer for as long as you can stand it (at least 20 minutes is recommended).
6. Serve and enjoy.
