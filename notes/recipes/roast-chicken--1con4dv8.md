## Ingredients
- 1 (3- or 4-pound) chicken
- About 1 tablespoon coarse kosher salt
- 1 lemon, or onion, or any fruit or vegetable for cavity

## Instructions
1. Turn your oven on high (450째F if you have ventilation, 425째F if not).
2. Coat chicken with coarse kosher salt so that you have an appealing crust of salt (a tablespoon or so).
3. Put the chicken in a pan.
4. Stick a lemon or some onion or any fruit or vegetable you have on hand into the cavity.
5. Put the chicken in the oven.
6. Go away for an hour.
7. When an hour has passed, take the chicken out of the oven and put it on the stove top or on a trivet for 15 more minutes.
8. Serve.
