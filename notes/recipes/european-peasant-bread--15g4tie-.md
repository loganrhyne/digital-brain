# European Peasant Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for European Peasant Bread.
- [claim] A rustic mixed-flour bread with rye and whole wheat.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 0.5 cup rye flour
- 0.5 cup whole wheat flour
- 5.5 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine lukewarm water and yeast, let sit until foamy.
2. Add salt and flours, mix until combined.
3. Rest for 40 minutes.
4. Shape loaves and place on baking sheet.
5. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 35 minutes until golden brown.
