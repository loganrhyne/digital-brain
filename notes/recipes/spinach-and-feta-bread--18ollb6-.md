# Spinach and Feta Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Spinach and Feta Bread.
- [claim] A savory bread with spinach and feta mixed into the dough.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 cup packed cooked spinach
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp granulated yeast
- 1 tbsp salt
- 2/3 cup crumbled feta
- 1.5 tbsp sugar
- 6.5 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine lukewarm water, yeast, and sugar, let sit until foamy.
2. Add spinach, salt, feta, and flour. Mix until combined.
3. Rest for 1 hour.
4. Shape loaves and place on baking sheet.
5. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
