# Zucchini Chocolate Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Zucchini Chocolate Cake.
- [claim] Combines chocolate and zucchini for a moist cake.
- [claim] Uses both cocoa and chocolate chips.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 125g butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 3 eggs
- 2 1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup yoghurt
- 1/4 cup cocoa
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp mixed spice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 3 cups grated zucchini
- 1 cup choc chips

## Instructions
1. Grease a baking pan.
2. Beat the butter and sugar until light and creamy.
3. Add the eggs one at a time with a spoonful of measured flour to prevent curdling.
4. Add the vanilla and yoghurt and mix well.
5. Sift the dry ingredients together.
6. Stir in with grated zucchini.
7. Pour into the prepared pan and sprinkle the surface with chocolate chips.
8. Bake at 175Â°C for 45 minutes.
