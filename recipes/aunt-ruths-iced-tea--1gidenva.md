# Aunt Ruth's Iced Tea

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Aunt Ruth's Iced Tea.
- [claim] Helen's family recipe for sweet mint iced tea.
- [claim] Uses Orange Pekoe loose tea.
- [claim] Steep tea for 10 minutes - must still be hot for mint to steep.
- [claim] Uses juice of 5 lemons (2/3 cup).
- [claim] Chill and serve with ice.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup Orange Pekoe loose tea
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 3 quarts water (12 cups)
- Juice of 5 lemons (2/3 cup)
- 4 sprigs of mint

## Instructions
1. Bring water to a full boil.
2. Make syrup with sugar and 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
3. Remove from heat and pour tea leaves into remaining water.
4. Let steep for 10 minutes (must still be hot for mint to steep).
5. Strain out tea and add sugar and mint.
6. Let cool and add lemon juice.
7. Chill and serve with ice.
