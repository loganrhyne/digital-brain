# Sun-Dried Tomato and Parmesan Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Sun-Dried Tomato and Parmesan Bread.
- [claim] Uses pre-made bread dough (1 pound).
- [claim] A savory Italian-style bread with tomatoes and cheese folded in.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 pound pre-mixed bread dough
- 0.5 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
- 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Roll out dough in 1/2 inch thick rectangle.
2. Spread sun-dried tomatoes and Parmesan cheese over dough.
3. Fold dough to incorporate tomatoes and cheese.
4. Shape into loaf and let rest for 1 hour.
5. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
