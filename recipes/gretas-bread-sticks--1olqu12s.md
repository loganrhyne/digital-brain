# Greta's Bread Sticks

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Greta's Bread Sticks.
- [claim] Helen's recipe for yeast bread sticks with beer.
- [claim] Water should be quite warm, then mixture should be lukewarm after adding oil and beer.
- [claim] Let rise until double before punching down.
- [claim] Dampen tops and sprinkle with salt before baking.
- [claim] Bake at 325-350°F until brown.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup oil
- 3/4 cup water
- 3/4 cup beer (room temperature)
- 4 1/2 cups flour
- 1 Tbsp yeast
- 1 tsp salt (plus more for topping)

## Instructions
1. Heat water until quite warm. Remove from heat and add oil and beer. Mixture should be lukewarm.
2. Add yeast and mix to dissolve.
3. Add salt and 3.5 cups flour. Mix until smooth.
4. Add remaining flour and mix.
5. Cover and let rise until double.
6. Punch down and make into sticks.
7. Dampen tops and sprinkle with salt.
8. Bake at 325-350°F until brown.
