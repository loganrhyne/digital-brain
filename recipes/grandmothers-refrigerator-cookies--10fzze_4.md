# Grandmother's Refrigerator Cookies

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Grandmother's Refrigerator Cookies.
- [claim] Family recipe - dough must chill overnight.
- [claim] Makes 4 rolls of dough.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 0.5 cup shortening
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cream of tartar
- 4 cups flour
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 2 tsp vanilla

## Instructions
1. Cream butter, shortening, and sugar.
2. Add beaten eggs.
3. Sift baking soda, cream of tartar, salt, and 1 cup flour together.
4. Mix well.
5. Add nuts, vanilla, and rest of flour.
6. Knead and roll into 4 long rolls.
7. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
8. Slice and bake at 375Â°F for 8 minutes. Let stand before removing.
