# Sticky Pecan Caramel Rolls

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Sticky Pecan Caramel Rolls.
- [claim] Uses pre-made enriched dough (Challah, Brioche, or Boule).
- [claim] Must release from pan immediately after baking.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 1.5 pounds Challah, Brioche, or Boule dough

### Topping
- 6 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 cup brown sugar
- 30 pecan halves

### Filling
- 4 tbsp salted butter, softened
- 0.25 cup sugar
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 0.25 tsp fresh nutmeg
- 0.5 cup chopped and toasted pecans
- Pinch of black pepper

## Instructions
1. Cream together butter, salt, and brown sugar for topping. Spread evenly over bottom of 9-inch cake pan and scatter pecan halves.
2. Roll dough into 1/8 inch thick rectangle.
3. Cream together butter, sugar, and spices for filling. Spread evenly over rolled-out dough and sprinkle with chopped nuts.
4. Roll dough into log.
5. Cut log into 8 equal pieces and arrange in pan with swirl facing up.
6. Cover with plastic wrap and rest for 1 hour.
7. Bake at 350Â°F for 40 minutes.
8. Immediately release from pan with knife (invert onto plate).
