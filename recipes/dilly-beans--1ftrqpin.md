# Dilly Beans

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Dilly Beans.
- [claim] Helen's recipe from Alan Zackheim via Paul.
- [claim] Put ingredients in jar, pack in beans, then fill with hot brine.
- [claim] Process jars after filling.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### In Each Pint Jar
- 1/2-1 head dill
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- 1 tsp mustard seeds

### Brine
- 3.5 cups water
- 3.5 cups white vinegar
- 4 Tbsp pickling salt
- Pinch red pepper flakes
- Pinch celery seed

### Plus
- Fresh green beans

## Instructions
1. Put dill, garlic, peppercorns, and mustard seeds in each pint jar.
2. Pack in beans.
3. Make brine: combine water, vinegar, pickling salt, red pepper flakes, and celery seed. Heat until hot.
4. Fill jars with hot brine.
5. Process jars according to standard canning procedures.
