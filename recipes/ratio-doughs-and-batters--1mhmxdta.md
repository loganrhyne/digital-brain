# Ratio - Doughs & Batters

## Observations
- [definition] Reference guide for dough and batter ratios.
- [claim] Based on Michael Ruhlman's Ratio book principles.
- [claim] All ratios are by weight (parts).
- [question] How do altitude adjustments affect these ratios?

## Ratios

### Bread (5:3 flour:liquid)
- Use 1/4 tsp yeast and 1/2 tsp salt per 5 oz (cup) flour
- Can add eggs to enrich, olive oil to flavor, honey to sweeten, wheat germ to fortify

### Pasta (3:2 flour:egg)
- Knead till smooth, rest at least 10 minutes before rolling
- For all-egg yolk pasta: same ratio plus a little olive oil and water if needed
- For pasta verde: 2:1 ratio plus 1/2 part blanched chopped spinach

### Pie Dough (3:2:1 flour:fat:liquid)
- Cut fat into flour, add just enough water to form dough, don't overwork
- For sweet: add 1 tbsp sugar per 5 oz flour
- For nut crust: add 1 cup pulverized nuts to 12 oz flour plus an egg

### Biscuit (3:2:1 flour:liquid:fat)
- Cut fat into flour, add liquid, don't overwork
- Add 1 tsp baking powder per 5 oz flour
- For flaky: roll out, fold in thirds, reroll; repeat 2 more times

### Cookie (3:2:1 flour:fat:sugar)
- Cream butter and sugar, add flour
- For drop cookies: equal parts sugar, flour, butter plus an egg per 8 oz flour
- Can add 1 tsp baking powder per 5 oz flour for leavening

### Pâte à Choux (1:2:2:1 flour:liquid:egg:fat)
- Bring water and fat to boil, stir in flour till dough forms, beat in eggs off heat
- Add Parmigiano-Reggiano for gougères
- For sweet: add 1 tbsp sugar per cup water

### Sponge/Pound Cake (1:1:1:1 flour:egg:fat:sugar)
- Pound cake: cream butter and sugar, mix in eggs, fold in flour
- Sponge: beat eggs and sugar till tripled, fold in flour, fold in butter
- Add 1 tsp baking powder per 5 oz flour for airier crumb

### Angel Food Cake (1:3:3 flour:egg:sugar)
- Egg whites only - no yolks, no fat
- Whip eggs and sugar to satiny peaks, fold in flour
- Add cream of tartar or lemon juice to stabilize
- Omit flour for all-purpose meringue

### Quickbread/Muffin (2:2:1:1 flour:liquid:egg:fat)
- Combine wet and dry separately, then mix
- Use 1 tsp baking powder per 5 oz flour
- For sweet: add 1 part sugar

### Fritter (2:2:1 flour:liquid:egg)
- Combine wet and dry separately, then mix
- Use 1 tsp baking powder and pinch of salt per 5 oz flour
- For sweet: add 1 tbsp sugar; for savory: add curry, coriander, cumin, chili

### Pancake (2:2:1:0.5 flour:liquid:egg:fat)
- Combine wet and dry separately, then mix
- Use 1 tsp baking powder, pinch of salt, 1 tbsp sugar per 5 oz flour
- Add vanilla for flavor

### Crepe (0.5:1:1 flour:liquid:egg)
- Combine wet and dry separately, then mix
- Vary liquid (water, milk, juice, stock) depending on use
- Season with fresh herbs or spices for savory dishes
