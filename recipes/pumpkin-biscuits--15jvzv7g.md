# Pumpkin Biscuits

## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 Tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
- 1/4 cup chilled butter, cut into small pieces
- 3/4 cup fat-free buttermilk
- 1/2 cup canned pumpkin

## Instructions
1. Spoon flour into dry measuring cups, and combine with ingredients through nutmeg.
2. Cut in chilled butter with a pastry blender or 2 knives until the mixture resembles coarse meal.
3. Combine buttermilk and pumpkin; add to flour mixture, stirring just until moist.
4. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface; knead lightly 5 times.
5. Roll out to about 1/2 inch thickness.
6. Cut into biscuits and place on a greased baking sheet.
7. Bake at 450°F for 11 minutes or until golden brown.
8. Serve hot.
