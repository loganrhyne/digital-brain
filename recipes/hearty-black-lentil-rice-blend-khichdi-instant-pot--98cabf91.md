# Hearty Black Lentil & Rice Blend Khichdi (Instant Pot)

## Ingredients
- 1 cup rice blend (brown/red/wild mix), rinsed
- 1/2 cup black lentils (beluga), rinsed
- 4 cups water or broth (for a porridge-y texture; use 3 cups if you want it thicker, more pilaf-like)
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 tomato, chopped (or 1/2 cup canned)
- 1 small piece ginger (1 inch), grated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1-2 green chilies (optional), slit
- 2 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2-1 tsp chili powder (to taste)
- salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Saute (Instant Pot on Saute mode):
Heat ghee/oil, add cumin seeds. When they sizzle, add bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, peppercorns.
2. Add onion saute until golden.
3. Add ginger, garlic, and green chili saute 1 min.
4. Add tomato + ground spices + salt cook 2-3 min until tomato softens.
5. Add grains and lentils:
Stir in rice blend + black lentils. Coat well with spices.
6. Add liquid:
Pour in 4 cups water/broth (more if you want soupier khichdi). Scrape the pot bottom to deglaze.
7. Pressure cook:
Seal lid. Pressure cook on High for 22 minutes.
Let Natural Release for 10 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.
