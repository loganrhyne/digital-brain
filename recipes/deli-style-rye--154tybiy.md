# Deli-Style Rye

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Deli-Style Rye bread.
- [claim] Uses a cornstarch wash for a shiny crust.
- [claim] Classic deli-style with caraway seeds.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tbsp caraway seeds (plus more for sprinkling)
- 1 cup rye flour
- 5.5 cups all-purpose flour

### Cornstarch Wash
- 0.5 tsp cornstarch
- 0.5 cup water

## Instructions
1. Combine lukewarm water, yeast, salt, caraway seeds, and flours. Mix until combined.
2. Rest for 40 minutes.
3. Make cornstarch wash: mix cornstarch with a little water to make paste, add remaining water, boil until glassy.
4. Shape loaves and place on baking sheet.
5. Paint top with cornstarch wash and sprinkle with caraway seeds.
6. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 30 minutes.
