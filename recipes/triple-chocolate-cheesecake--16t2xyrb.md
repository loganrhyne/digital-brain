# Triple Chocolate Cheesecake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Triple Chocolate Cheesecake.
- [claim] No-bake cheesecake with three types of chocolate.
- [claim] Requires overnight refrigeration.
- [claim] Cut with warm knife for clean slices.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Base
- 200g plain chocolate biscuits
- 80g butter, melted

### Filling
- 1/3 cup cold tap water
- 5 tsp gelatine
- 500g cream cheese, softened
- 0.5 cup icing sugar
- 0.5 cup milk
- 150g white chocolate, melted
- 150g dark chocolate, melted
- 1.5 cups thickened cream, whipped

### Topping
- 100g milk chocolate, grated
- Cocoa powder

## Instructions
1. Grease and line a 24cm springform pan.
2. Process biscuits to fine crumbs. Mix with melted butter. Press into base of pan. Refrigerate 20 minutes.
3. Place water in heatproof bowl, sprinkle gelatine. Stand 1 minute. Microwave 20-40 seconds until dissolved. Set aside 15 minutes.
4. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and milk until smooth. Stir in gelatine.
5. Divide mixture between 2 bowls.
6. Stir white chocolate into one, dark chocolate into other.
7. Fold half the whipped cream into each mixture.
8. Pour dark chocolate mixture over base. Freeze 10 minutes until firm to touch.
9. Carefully spread white chocolate mixture over dark layer.
10. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
11. Release sides of pan. Top with grated milk chocolate and dust with cocoa.
