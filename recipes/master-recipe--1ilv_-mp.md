# Master Recipe (Basic Artisan Bread)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Master Recipe - basic artisan bread.
- [claim] From "Artisan Bread in Five Minutes a Day" style.
- [claim] Makes enough dough for multiple loaves - can be stored and used over time.
- [claim] Includes scaled-up batch notes (15 cups water, 32.5 cups flour).
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Standard Batch
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp granulated yeast (1.5 packets)
- 1.5 tbsp coarse kosher or sea salt
- 6.5 cups unsifted, unbleached, all-purpose white flour

### Large Batch (5x)
- 15 cups water (3.6L)
- 7.5 tbsp yeast
- 7.5 tbsp salt
- 32.5 cups flour (7.7L)

## Instructions
1. Combine water and yeast in large container. Let sit briefly.
2. Add salt and flour. Mix until no dry flour remains.
3. Cover loosely and let rise at room temperature for 2 hours (or until doubled).
4. Refrigerate and use as needed - dough improves over first few days.
