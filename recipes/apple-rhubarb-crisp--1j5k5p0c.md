# Apple-Rhubarb Crisp

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Apple-Rhubarb Crisp.
- [claim] Source: Bon Appétit, March 1996.
- [claim] Oats, brown sugar and walnuts team up with cinnamon and cloves in the streusel topping.
- [claim] Topping can be made 1 day ahead and chilled.
- [claim] Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Topping
- 3/4 cup all purpose flour
- 3/4 cup (packed) golden brown sugar
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- 6 tablespoons (3/4 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts

### Filling
- 1 1/2 pounds Golden Delicious apples, peeled, cored, cut into 1/2-inch pieces (about 4 cups)
- 3/4 pound rhubarb, cut into 1/2-inch pieces (about 3 cups)
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons all purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Vanilla ice cream or frozen yogurt for serving

## Instructions

### Make Topping
1. Mix flour, brown sugar, oats, cinnamon, and cloves in medium bowl.
2. Rub in butter until mixture begins to clump together.
3. Mix in nuts. (Can be made 1 day ahead and chilled.)

### Make Filling and Bake
4. Preheat oven to 400°F.
5. Combine apples, rhubarb, sugar, flour and vanilla extract in large bowl and toss to coat.
6. Transfer apple mixture to 8x8x2-inch glass baking dish.
7. Sprinkle topping evenly over fruit.
8. Bake until fruit is tender when pierced with knife and topping is crisp, about 45 minutes. Cover with foil if topping is browning too quickly.
9. Cool 20 minutes. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
