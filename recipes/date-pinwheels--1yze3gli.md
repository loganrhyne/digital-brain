## Ingredients

### Dough
- 2/3 cup butter
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/3 cups packed brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Filling
- 2 1/4 cups chopped pitted dates
- 1 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup water
- 3/4 cup finely chopped nuts

## Instructions
1. Beat butter with mixer for 30 seconds. Add half the flour.
2. Stir in brown sugar, eggs, baking soda, and salt. Beat thoroughly.
3. Stir in remaining flour.
4. Divide dough in half, cover, and chill about 1 hour or until firm enough to handle.
5. Combine dates, sugar, and 3/4 cup water in a saucepan. Bring to boiling, reduce heat.
6. Simmer uncovered for about 5 minutes or until thick. Stir in nuts and let cool.
7. Roll each half of dough between lightly floured waxed paper sheets; should make two 12x10 inch rectangles.
8. Remove top sheets and spread half the filling over each rectangle to within 1/2 inch of the edge.
9. Roll up from a long side, removing bottom sheet of paper.
10. Wrap in plastic wrap. Chill for 4 to 48 hours.
11. Cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices. Place 2 inches apart on lightly greased cookie sheets.
12. Bake at 375째F (190째C) for 10-12 minutes or until lightly brown.
13. Cool on sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to cooling racks and let cool completely.
