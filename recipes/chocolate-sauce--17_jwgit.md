# Chocolate Sauce

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Chocolate Sauce.
- [claim] Helen's simple chocolate sauce.
- [claim] Bring to rolling boil until sauce thickens (5-10 minutes).
- [claim] Store in refrigerator.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 squares unsweetened chocolate (2 oz, 29 grams)
- 1/2 to 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup water or skim milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions
1. Combine unsweetened chocolate, sugar, and milk in a saucepan.
2. Heat on stovetop to melt chocolate gradually.
3. Bring to rolling boil until the sauce thickens (5-10 minutes).
4. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla.
5. Store in refrigerator.
