# 40 Cloves and a Chicken

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for 40 Cloves and a Chicken.
- [claim] Simple roasted chicken with 40 cloves of garlic.
- [claim] Brown chicken on both sides in skillet before baking.
- [claim] Bake covered at 350°F for 1.5 hours.
- [claim] Let rest for 5-10 minutes before carving.
- [claim] Uses fresh thyme and olive oil for flavor.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken (broiler/fryer) cut into 8 pieces
- 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 10 sprigs fresh thyme
- 40 peeled cloves garlic
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
3. Toss chicken with 2 tablespoons olive oil and brown on both sides in a wide fry pan or skillet over high heat.
4. Remove from heat, add remaining 1/2 cup oil, thyme, and garlic cloves.
5. Cover and bake for 1 1/2 hours.
6. Remove chicken from the oven, let rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
7. Carve and serve.
