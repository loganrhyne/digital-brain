# Broa (Portuguese Corn Bread)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Broa (Portuguese Corn Bread).
- [claim] Traditional Portuguese bread with cornmeal.
- [claim] Can use stone-ground or standard cornmeal.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 3 cups lukewarm water
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 cups stone-ground or standard cornmeal
- 5.5 cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions
1. Combine water, yeast, salt, cornmeal, and flour. Mix until combined.
2. Form into flattened free-form loaves.
3. Rest for 40 minutes.
4. Bake at 450Â°F with steam for 30 minutes.
