# No Bake Cookies

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for No Bake Cookies.
- [claim] Helen's recipe for no-bake chocolate peanut butter oatmeal cookies.
- [claim] Boil for 1.5 minutes exactly.
- [claim] Work quickly after removing from heat as mixture sets fast.
- [claim] Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper and let cool until hardened.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup butter (113 grams)
- 4 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup crunchy peanut butter
- 3 cups quick-cooking oats
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions
1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa.
2. Bring to a boil for 1.5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and immediately stir in vanilla and peanut butter.
4. Quickly add oats one cup at a time.
5. Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper.
6. Let cool until hardened.
