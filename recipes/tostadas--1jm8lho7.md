# Tostadas

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Tostadas.
- [claim] Helen's recipe for tostada meat filling.
- [claim] Boil gently for 15 minutes before adding beans and milk.
- [claim] Can use half can of tomato sauce instead of full water amount.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Meat Filling
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 cup chopped onion
- 1 pkg taco sauce mix
- 1 1/2 cups water (or half a can of tomato sauce)
- 1 cup refried beans
- 1/2 cup undiluted evaporated milk

### Toppings
- Tortilla chips
- Lettuce
- Chopped tomatoes
- Cheddar cheese
- Olives
- Green onions

## Instructions
1. Brown ground beef and onions.
2. Stir in taco sauce mix, water, and tomato sauce.
3. Boil gently for 15 minutes.
4. Stir in beans and milk.
5. Heat and serve with toppings.
