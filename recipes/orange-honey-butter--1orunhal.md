# Orange Honey Butter

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Orange Honey Butter.
- [claim] Helen's simple compound butter spread.
- [claim] Beat with mixer at medium speed until well blended.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup softened butter
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/2 tsp grated orange rind

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl.
2. Beat with a mixer at medium speed until well blended.
