# Maddy's Mommy's Brownies

## Ingredients
- 6 oz unsweetened chocolate (170g, as close to unsweetened as possible)
- 3/4 cup unsalted butter (170g)
- 3 large eggs (4-5 depending on size)
- 2 1/4 cups sugar (2.5 cups)
- 1 cup all-purpose flour (1.3 cups)
- 1/4 tsp salt (1/2 tsp)
- 2 tsp vanilla (3 tsp or 6 packets)
- 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions
1. Melt the chocolate and butter over low heat in a small heavy saucepan, stirring until mixture is smooth. Let cool completely.
2. In a large bowl with an electric mixer beat the eggs and add sugar a little at a time.
3. Beat the mixture at high speed for 7 or 8 minutes or until it is very thick and pale.
4. Stir in the chocolate mixture, flour, salt, and vanilla. Blend well and stir in nuts.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and floured 9-inch-square baking pan.
6. Smooth the top and bake at 375°F for 30 to 40 minutes or until it pulls away slightly from the sides of the pan.
