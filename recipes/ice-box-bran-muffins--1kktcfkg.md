# Ice Box Bran Muffins

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Ice Box Bran Muffins.
- [claim] Helen's refrigerator muffin batter recipe.
- [claim] Batter will keep in refrigerator for up to six weeks.
- [claim] Hydrate 100% bran with boiling liquid (water + prune juice or all water).
- [claim] Can use Grape Nuts instead of bran nibs.
- [claim] Bake at 400째F (200째C) for 15-20 minutes.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 cups 100-percent bran (or 2 cups Grape Nuts) - nibs, to be hydrated
- 2 cups boiling liquid (1.5 cups water + 0.5 cups prune juice)
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups white sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 quart buttermilk
- 5 cups flour
- 5 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 cups All Bran (sticks)
- 1 lb dates and nuts

## Instructions
1. Mix together the 100% bran and boiling liquid.
2. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and buttermilk.
3. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt into wet mixture.
4. Stir in All Bran and first bran mixture - batter will be thick.
5. Stir in dates and nuts before putting batter into containers for refrigeration.
6. Store in refrigerator (will keep up to six weeks).
7. When ready to bake, fill greased muffin tins and bake for 15 to 20 minutes at 400째F (200째C).
