# Chicken Noodle Soup

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Chicken Noodle Soup.
- [claim] Simple and quick soup ready in about 15 minutes.
- [claim] Bring stock to boil for 2 minutes with lid on.
- [claim] Simmer onion, celery, and garlic for 2 minutes.

- [claim] Add herbs off heat.

- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 4 cups chicken stock, home made or store bought
- 3/4 cup diced onion
- 3/4 cup diced celery
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 2 ounces dried egg noodles, cooked to al dente
- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped fresh tarragon leaves
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh parsley leaves
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Lemon halves, for serving

## Instructions
1. Bring stock to boil for 2 minutes in a large, non-reactive stockpot with lid on, over high heat.
2. Add onion, celery, and garlic. Lower heat and simmer for 2 minutes.
3. Add noodles and cook 5 more minutes.
4. Remove from heat and add herbs and salt and pepper, to taste.
5. Serve with lemon halves and add squeeze of lemon juice if desired.
