# Sunflower Seed Breakfast Loaf

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Sunflower Seed Breakfast Loaf.
- [claim] A hearty breakfast bread with honey and sunflower seeds.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 2 cups lukewarm water
- 0.5 cup honey
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tbsp yeast
- 4 tbsp sunflower oil
- 3 eggs
- 6 cups bread flour
- 1 cup sunflower seeds

## Instructions
1. Combine water, honey, sugar, salt, yeast, oil, eggs, flour, and sunflower seeds. Mix until smooth dough forms.
2. Place in greased loaf pan.
3. Rest for 1 hour and 40 minutes.
4. Bake at 375Â°F for 35-40 minutes until golden brown.
