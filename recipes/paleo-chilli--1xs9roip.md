# Paleo Chilli

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Paleo Chilli.
- [claim] Simple paleo-friendly chili with ground beef and vegetables.
- [claim] Uses olives for fat content instead of beans.
- [claim] 1/2 cup contains approximately 1 block protein, 1/2 block carbohydrate, and 1 block fat (Zone diet).
- [claim] Eat an additional 1/2 block of carbohydrate for a complete 1-block meal.
- [claim] Low-carb, grain-free recipe.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb ground beef
- 3 cups onion, chopped
- ~80 olives, chopped
- 4 cups zucchini, chopped
- 2 cups salsa

## Instructions
1. Chop onions, zucchini and olives.
2. Brown ground beef in skillet.
3. Saut√© onions and zucchini in skillet.
4. Mix ground beef, olives, zucchini and onion together in a large stock pot.
5. Pour salsa into chili and stir well.
