## Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2/3 cup (150g) butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.
2. Beat butter, brown sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until creamy.
3. Add eggs (beaten).
4. Gradually stir in flour mixture.
5. Spread in greased pan and sprinkle chocolate chips evenly on top.
6. Bake at 350째F/177째C for 25 minutes.
