# Grandmother's Banana Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Grandmother's Banana Bread.
- [claim] Family recipe using dried buttermilk.
- [claim] Uses old (very ripe) bananas.
- [claim] Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 3/4 cup plain flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 Tbsp dried buttermilk
- 3 old bananas
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1/4 cup water (if using dried buttermilk)
- 2 eggs

## Instructions
1. Blend shortening and sugar.
2. Mix in flour, baking soda, and buttermilk.
3. Mix water and vanilla.
4. Add in eggs.
5. Add in mashed bananas.
6. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
