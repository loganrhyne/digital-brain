# Dense and Firm Cheesecake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Dense and Firm Cheesecake.
- [claim] Source: Published September 1995.
- [claim] Center should jiggle like Jell-O when done.
- [claim] Can be refrigerated up to 4 days.
- [claim] Must scrape bowl after each egg to avoid lumps.

## Ingredients
- Butter for pan
- Graham cracker crumbs for pan
- Cream cheese
- Sugar
- Eggs
- Lemon zest
- Vanilla
- Heavy cream
- Sour cream

## Instructions
1. Adjust oven rack to middle position, heat oven to 500°F.
2. Line bottom of 9-inch springform pan with foil, tuck underneath, assemble, pull foil around sides.
3. Brush bottom and sides with butter. Sprinkle crumbs over bottom, tilt to coat evenly.
4. Beat cream cheese until smooth.
5. Gradually add sugar, beat on medium speed until dissolved, about 3 minutes.
6. Add eggs one at a time, beating until just incorporated. Scrape down after each addition.
7. Add zest and vanilla, beat until just incorporated.
8. Remove from mixer, stir in cream and sour cream.
9. Pour into prepared pan.
10. Bake at 500°F for 10 minutes.
11. Reduce to 200°F, leaving door ajar until temperature drops.
12. Bake until perimeter is set but center jiggles, about 1 hour longer.
13. Cool to room temperature on wire rack.
14. Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours.
