# Apple and Pear Coffee Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Apple and Pear Coffee Cake.
- [claim] Uses brioche dough (page 189) and streusel topping.
- [claim] 1 pound of brioche dough is a grapefruit-size portion.
- [claim] Uses decreased amount of dough compared to some recipes.
- [claim] Requires 1 hour 20 minutes rest before baking.
- [claim] Bake at 375°F for about 45 minutes until tester comes out clean.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Streusel Topping
- 1 cup oats
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup brown sugar, well packed
- 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
- 1 cup melted butter
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

### Cake
- 1 pound brioche dough
- 2 small apples (1 tart and 1 sweet, thinly sliced)
- 1 large pear, thinly sliced
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- Zest of half an orange
- 1 1/2 cups streusel topping (from above)

## Instructions

### Make Streusel
1. Place all streusel ingredients in a bowl and toss until well combined.

### Assemble Cake
2. Preheat oven to 375°F.
3. Prepare an 8-inch x 3-inch round cake pan or 8-inch nonstick springform with grease, a round piece of parchment, and a dusting of flour or sugar.
4. Split the pound of brioche dough in two equal pieces, form into balls and roll out to be just slightly wider than the cake pan.
5. Place one disk of dough in the prepared pan. It should come up the sides just a bit.
6. Combine the apples, pear, brown sugar and zest in a bowl.
7. Spread half of the apple mixture on the first layer of dough.
8. Cover with about 3/4 cup streusel.
9. Repeat with other layer of dough, rest of apples and another 3/4 cup streusel.
10. Let the cake rest for 1 hour and 20 minutes.

### Bake
11. Bake for about 45 minutes, until tester comes out of the center clean.
12. Run a sharp knife around the edge of the cake.
13. If using cake pan, invert onto a plate, lift pan off cake.
14. Peel parchment paper off bottom of cake and invert onto cake plate.
