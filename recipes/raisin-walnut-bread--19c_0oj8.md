# Raisin-Walnut Bread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Raisin-Walnut Bread.
- [claim] Uses pre-made Oatmeal Bread dough as base.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1.5 pounds Oatmeal Bread dough
- 1 cup raisins
- 0.75 cups walnuts

### Egg Wash
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 tbsp water

## Instructions
1. Roll dough into 1/2-inch thick rectangle.
2. Sprinkle raisins and walnuts over dough.
3. Fold into loaf shape to incorporate fruit and nuts.
4. Place in greased loaf pan.
5. Rest for 1 hour 40 minutes.
6. Brush with egg wash.
7. Bake at 350Â°F for 45 minutes.
