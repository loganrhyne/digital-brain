# Rebecca's Peach Pie

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Rebecca's Peach Pie.
- [claim] Family recipe with homemade crust.
- [claim] Cook until juice looks thick.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Crust
- 1.75 cups flour
- 7/8 cup shortening
- 1 tsp salt
- 6-7 tbsp cold water

### Filling
- Fresh peaches, peeled and sliced
- 2/3 cup white sugar
- 2 tbsp butter, chopped

## Instructions

### Make Crust
1. Cut shortening into flour and salt.
2. Start with 3-4 tbsp water and mix in with fork.
3. Add water until dough comes away from side of bowl.
4. Roll out on pastry cloth.

### Assemble and Bake
5. Fill crust with sliced peaches.
6. Sprinkle sugar over peaches.
7. Dot with chopped butter.
8. Add top crust if desired.
9. Bake at 425°F for 10 minutes.
10. Reduce to 350°F and bake 20-25 more minutes until juice looks thick.
