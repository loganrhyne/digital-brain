## Ingredients

### Zucchini Layer
- 2 large zucchini
- 1 tablespoon salt

### Meat Sauce
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 small green bell pepper, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 cup tomato paste
- 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1/4 cup red wine
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
- Hot water as needed

### Cheese Filling
- 1 egg
- 1 (15 ounce) container low-fat ricotta cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

### Additional Layers
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
- 8 ounces grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325째F. Grease a deep 9x13 inch baking pan.
2. Slice zucchini lengthwise into very thin slices. Sprinkle slices lightly with salt; set aside to drain in a colander.
3. To prepare the meat sauce, cook and stir ground beef and black pepper in a large skillet over medium-high heat for 5 minutes.
4. Add in green pepper and onion; cook and stir until meat is no longer pink.
5. Stir in tomato paste, tomato sauce, wine, basil, and oregano, adding a small amount of hot water if sauce is too thick.
6. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer sauce for about 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
7. Meanwhile, stir egg, ricotta, and parsley together in a bowl until well combined.
8. To assemble lasagna, spread 1/2 of the meat sauce into the bottom of prepared pan.
9. Layer 1/2 the zucchini slices, 1/2 the ricotta mixture, all of the spinach, followed by all of the mushrooms, then 1/2 the mozzarella cheese.
10. Repeat by layering the remaining meat sauce, zucchini slices, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella.
11. Spread Parmesan cheese evenly over the top; cover with foil.
12. Bake for 45 minutes.
13. Remove foil; raise oven temperature to 350째F, and bake an additional 15 minutes.
14. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
