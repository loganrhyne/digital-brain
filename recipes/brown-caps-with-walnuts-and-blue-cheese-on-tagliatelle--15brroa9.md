# Brown Caps with Walnuts and Blue Cheese on Tagliatelle

## Ingredients
- 200g Brown Cap mushrooms
- 1 clove garlic
- Salt and pepper
- 50g butter
- 2 fresh sage leaves, finely sliced
- 250ml cream
- 100g walnut pieces, chopped
- 500g fresh tagliatelle
- 100g crumbly blue cheese
- Olive oil (for pasta)
- Grated Parmesan (for serving)

## Instructions
1. Crush garlic and salt into a paste.
2. Melt butter in large frying pan and add paste.
3. Fry until golden.
4. Add mushrooms and toss to coat.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Reduce heat, pour in cream and add walnuts.
7. Stir together and simmer till reduced by 1/3 (about 10 minutes).
8. Boil pasta, drain, return, toss with olive oil and pepper.
9. Crumble blue cheese through the simmering cream and remove from heat just as cheese starts to melt.
10. Serve pasta with mushroom sauce and grated Parmesan.
