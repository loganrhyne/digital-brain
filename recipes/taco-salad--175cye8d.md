# Taco Salad

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Taco Salad.
- [claim] Uses organic, grass-fed ground beef.
- [claim] Includes homemade guacamole recipe.
- [claim] Prepare guacamole ahead of time and chill while making salad.
- [claim] Lime juice in guacamole is reactive with metal - avoid metal mixing bowls and spoons.
- [claim] Pour lime juice over avocado right away to prevent browning.
- [claim] Can top with black olives, onion, chives, or other fresh vegetables.
- [claim] Can use baby spinach or blend of lettuce and spinach as salad base.
- [claim] Something special about warm ground beef on top of crisp romaine.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Taco Meat
- 2 lbs organic, grass-fed ground beef (fresh, or frozen and thawed)
- 1 can (16 oz) organic diced tomatoes, or dice your own
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp cumin

### Salad
- 1 head fresh, crisp romaine lettuce, chopped
- 1 each yellow, green and red pepper, diced
- 1 jar organic salsa (or make your own)

### Guacamole
- 4 fresh avocado, pitted and mashed
- 1-2 garlic cloves, pressed
- 2 limes, squeezed for juice
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp sea salt
- Fresh ground black pepper, to taste
- Fresh cilantro (chopped), to taste

## Instructions

### Make Guacamole
1. Mash the avocado in a glass or ceramic bowl to desired consistency.
2. Pour the lime juice over the avocado right away to prevent browning.
3. Use a wooden spoon to mix in all other ingredients.
4. Chill while you make the salad.

### Make Taco Salad
1. Start to brown the ground beef.
2. Add tomatoes and spices and simmer until it's no longer pink.
3. Drop a pile of chopped romaine on a plate.
4. Top with ground beef.
5. Sprinkle with diced pepper and cover with fresh salsa.
6. Spoon a hearty helping of guacamole on the side.
