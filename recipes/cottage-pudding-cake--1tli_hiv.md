# Cottage Pudding Cake

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Cottage Pudding Cake.
- [claim] Helen's simple vanilla cake.
- [claim] Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes until cake pulls away from sides.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg
- 3/4 cup milk

## Instructions
1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a small bowl.
2. Mix oil, granulated sugar, milk, vanilla and the beaten egg in a large bowl.
3. Gradually stir in flour mixture.
4. Pour batter into greased 8x8 pan.
5. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until cake pulls away from sides.
