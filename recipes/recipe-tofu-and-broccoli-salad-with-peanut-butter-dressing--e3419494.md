# Recipe: Tofu and Broccoli Salad with Peanut Butter Dressing

- [definition] Recipe for Recipe: Tofu and Broccoli Salad with Peanut Butter Dressing.
- [claim] Source: https://www.thekitchn.com/recipe-peanut-butter-broccoli-and-tofu-salad-232445.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
- 1/4 cup rice vinegar
- 3 tablespoons tamari or soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 to 2 teaspoons sriracha hot sauce (optional)
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 12 ounces broccoli slaw (no dressing)
- 1 medium red bell pepper, julienned
- 1 pound baked tofu (see recipe notes), cut into small cubes
- 1/4 cup shelled and cooked edamame
- 1/4 cup roasted peanuts
- 1/4 cup loosely packed fresh cilantro leaves

## Instructions
1. Place the peanut butter, rice vinegar, tamari or soy sauce, water, Sriracha (if using), and sesame oil in a bowl and whisk until smooth; set aside.
2. Place the broccoli slaw and bell pepper in a large bowl and toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
3. When ready to serve, divide the slaw mixture between 4 plates. Top with the baked tofu, peanuts, and cilantro. Drizzle with the peanut sauce.
