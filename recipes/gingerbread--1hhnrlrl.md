# Gingerbread

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Gingerbread.
- [claim] Helen's classic gingerbread cake recipe.
- [claim] All ingredients should be at room temperature (about 70°F).
- [claim] Can add 3 tablespoons finely chopped crystallized ginger if desired.
- [claim] Bake at 350°F for about 1 hour until toothpick comes out clean.
- [claim] Let cool in pan on rack.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Dry Ingredients
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Liquid Ingredients
- 1 cup hot water
- 1/2 cup molasses
- 1/2 cup honey

### Butter Mixture
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and cooled
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 cup sugar

### Optional
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped crystallized ginger

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a 9-inch square baking pan or line the bottom with wax or parchment paper.
2. Whisk together dry ingredients thoroughly.
3. Whisk together liquid ingredients (hot water, molasses, honey) in a small bowl.
4. Whisk together butter mixture (melted butter, egg, sugar) in a large bowl.
5. Add the dry and liquid ingredients alternately to the butter mixture, whisking well after each addition.
6. Add crystallized ginger if desired.
7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
8. Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.
9. Let the pan cool on a rack for 10 minutes. Let the cake cool in the pan on a rack.
