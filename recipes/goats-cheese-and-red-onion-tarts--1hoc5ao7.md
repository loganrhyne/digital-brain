# Goat's Cheese and Red Onion Tarts

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Goat's Cheese and Red Onion Tarts.
- [claim] Individual tarts made in a muffin pan with filo pastry.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients
- Cooking oil
- 3 red onions, thinly sliced
- 2 tsp honey
- 1 tsp chopped fresh thyme
- 4 sheets filo pastry dough
- 75g goat cheese, crumbled

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F), oil muffin pan.
2. Cook onions until soft.
3. Add honey and thyme and cook for 3 minutes more.
4. Stack pastry and cut into circles.
5. Fit discs into muffin tin and bake for 5 minutes.
6. Top with onion and cheese mix and bake for 8 minutes more.
