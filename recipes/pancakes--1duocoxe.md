# Pancakes (with Instant Mix)

## Observations
- [definition] Recipe for Pancakes with homemade instant mix.
- [claim] Mix keeps for 3 months in lidded container.
- [claim] Griddle is ready when water drops dance across surface.
- [claim] Can hold pancakes in 200°F oven for 20-30 minutes.
- [question] What substitutions work well?

## Ingredients

### Instant Pancake Mix (makes multiple batches)
- 6 cups all-purpose flour (720g)
- 1.5 tsp baking soda (check expiration date)
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp sugar

### Per Batch of Pancakes
- 2 eggs, separated
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 4 tbsp (60g) melted butter
- 2 cups Instant Pancake Mix
- 1 stick butter for greasing pan
- 2 cups fresh fruit (blueberries, etc.) if desired

## Instructions

### Make the Mix
1. Combine all mix ingredients in a lidded container. Shake to mix.
2. Use within 3 months.

### Make Pancakes
1. Heat electric griddle or frying pan to 350°F. Heat oven to 200°F.
2. Whisk egg whites and buttermilk in a small bowl.
3. In another bowl, whisk egg yolks with melted butter.
4. Combine buttermilk mixture with egg yolk mixture, whisk until combined.
5. Pour liquid ingredients on top of pancake mix. Mix just enough to bring together - don't work out all lumps.
6. Lightly butter griddle, wipe off thoroughly with paper towel (no butter visible).
7. Gently ladle batter onto griddle, sprinkle on fruit if desired.
8. When bubbles set around edges and bottom is golden, gently flip.
9. Cook 2-3 more minutes until set.
10. Serve immediately or hold on towel-lined baking sheet in warm oven.
